This site is a personal project. The main objective is to create a Pokedex where you can search pokemons by its Id or name (soon I hope so). Also, you can test your knowlegde about Pokemons by taking the Quiz in the Quiz section.

With the Pokedex you can search for Pokemons and learn about them. There are many things to discover about pokemons. Find out the evolution path, look at the moves it can lear and much more!
Go find a Pokemon!

To take the test go to the Quiz section. There you can challenge yourself getting the right answer for the different questions. An excellent little game for the nostalgia. You can invite your friends and find out who is the real pokemon master!
